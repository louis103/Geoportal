A geoportal is a gateway to web-based geospatial resources, enabling you to discover, view, and access geospatial information and services made available by their providing organizations. Likewise, data providers can use the geoportal to make their geospatial resources discoverable, viewable, and accessible to others.

A geoportal that is publicly accessible can be used by Humanitarian Organizations, Local Governments, National Governments, Gis Specialists, Students on research projects,Remote Sensing specialists,Cartographers, NGOs, Property Developers and also in Emergency response teams. Above all, a Geoportal comes handy during data hunting since it offers a centralized location and availability of spatial datasets.

Absolutely Yes. If you want to share your datasets you've used in own projects, you can send us an email at lowasoftwaresltd@gmail.com then the administrator of the geoportal will publish the dataset.

Once on the geoportal, click on the 'Datasets' link on the header. This opens a new page with different datasets, here you can Search dataset(s),Filter dataset(s) or Order certain dataset(s) for easier location. Once you see a dataset you like,click on View Dataset button to reveal the metadata. On the new page, you can either download dataset, or download metadata. What is more advisable is downloading both the dataset and metadata so as to have a easy time working with the dataset.
